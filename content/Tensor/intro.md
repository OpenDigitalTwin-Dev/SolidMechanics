## 张量运算

:::{admonition} $A:B=A_{ij}B_{ij}=\text{tr}(AB^{T})=\text{tr}(A^{T}B)=\text{tr}(B^{T}A)$
:class: tip, dropdown

$
A_{ij}B_{ij} = A_{ij}B^{T}_{ji} = \text{tr}(AB^{T})
$

且

$
\text{tr}(A) = \text{tr}(A^{T})
$

:::

:::{admonition} $A:BC=(B^{T}A):C$
:class: tip, dropdown

$
A:BC = \text{tr}(C^{T}B^{T}A) = (B^{T}A) : C
$

:::
